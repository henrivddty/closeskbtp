Yorkshire Terriers

Are fun and easy to keep dogs. Their weight does not exceed 3 kilograms, height-15-20 centimeters. Dogs are distinguished by a long soft coat, but they almost do not shed. Yorks are easy to train, can get used to any living conditions. Suitable for frequent travelers – thanks to their compactness and adaptability, they will become good travel companions.
These dogs do not have to walk every day – they learn to go to the toilet for a diaper or in the tray. Yorkshire Terriers require constant attention to themselves and careful health control.

Pomeranian

These small dogs look like fluffy balls. They fall in love with an unusual appearance and easy character. Spitz can be trained, learn the basic commands, get used to the way of life of their owners. They are loyal, hardy, adaptable to any climate. Dogs easily get along with other Pets and people. The long furry coat of Spitz requires careful care and regular combing, especially during the period of abundant molting. In addition, they often bark loudly, and if the apartment does not have sound insulation, this can lead to complaints from neighbors.

Pug dogs

Are charismatic and fun decorative dogs for apartments. They are easy to teach teams and are intelligent and quick-witted.
Pugs can become good friends – they are sensitive to the emotions of the owner, show compassion. They are well suited to life in a confined space, because they do not like activity, rarely run and tend to a quiet lifestyle. Because of this, you need to carefully monitor their nutrition to prevent obesity. Also, pug dogs need to be regularly examined by a veterinarian – they are not in very good health.

French bulldog

French bulldogs are compact dogs with a height of 25-30 centimeters. They have a flattened nose and cute creases around their eyes and nose. A strong physique and proportionate voice gives the bulldog an attractive appearance. These dogs are very smart and loyal, quickly become attached to the owner, accompany him on trips and travels. Do not require daily walking and are accustomed to the tray, but need to walk once every 2-3 days. Bulldogs need to be regularly bathed, combed and monitored for the condition of their claws, eyes and ears. They should not be allowed to run too much – because of the structure of the nose, they have breathing problems.

Pekingese

The Pekingese is a dog with a cheerful, balanced character and fun appearance. They have a long thick coat and a small funny face. They get along well in the apartment, easily get used to the tray and diaper. Often you should walk with them only in spring and autumn at a temperature of 20-25 degrees, otherwise they can overheat or catch a cold. Their long hair should be carefully combed and trimmed. Haircuts also require claws and hairs between the fingers. Pekingese do not get along with other Pets and children, often barking loudly and making a mess in the apartment.

Chihuahua

Chihuahua-dogs with a funny and balanced character. Their size does not exceed 25 centimeters, the body is proportional, the hair is short. They are happy to play with children, become attached to their owners and do not require constant attention. Sneezes do not need a lot of space, but they must have their own territory – a small mattress or a bed in a quiet corner. They are not prone to excessive activity and can stay at home for several days, but once every 3-4 days you must go out with them. On walks, the dog should be carefully monitored – on the hands to carry through puddles, stairs, high curbs.
